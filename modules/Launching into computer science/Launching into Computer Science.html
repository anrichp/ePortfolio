Learning Outcomes

  1. Explore the professional roles, skillset, and ethical responsibilities of computer science professionals.
  2. Identify and explain the architecture, structure and functionality of basic components of computer system, including the representation of data.
  3. Critically evaluate the functionality of different types of software, i.e., operating system, utility programmes, languages and applications.
  4. Identify and appraise the different types of networks, including internets and intranets.
  5. Critically appraise the emerging trends in the field, such as cloud computing, big data, cyber security, and the professional and ethical requirements for dealing with such contemporary computer-based technologies.
